# Philosophy

## Core Ideas

> The most important skill for an IT professional is learning how to learn.

> The most important skill for a programmer is the ability to break a problem into smaller pieces.

> In today's world, especially IT, a person's advantage is their ability to learn new skills, not what they already know.

## Job Levels

Intern → Junior → Mid → Senior → Expert → Master

Each level is not just about technical depth — it's about:

- **Intern**: Learn, observe, follow instructions
- **Junior**: Work independently on small tasks, ask good questions
- **Mid**: Own features end-to-end, mentor juniors
- **Senior**: Design systems, make architectural decisions
- **Expert**: Solve novel problems, influence industry direction
- **Master**: Create new paradigms, shape the field
